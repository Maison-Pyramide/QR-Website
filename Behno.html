BEHNO

Accessories

Collection 8 is inspired by our Nashik, a city in India where the behno team spends a lot of time. It is vibrant and colorful and sits on the bed of a holy body of water, hence the wave quilting detail.
The behno team enjoys much of its citric offering and incorporated notes of lemon, orange, and lime in the color palette for the season along with adding in stamped exotic textures of lizard and croc.
Mashing up this color palette with an influence of Franz West’s signature work, behno puts forward a modern embroidery program for SS20 done on its two new iconic shapes, the Mary Bag Mini and the Elizabeth Baguette.
