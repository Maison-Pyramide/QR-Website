The collection captures the essence of Angely’s happy childhood memories of her favorite desserts. She quotes her happy place as being the kitchen; surrounded by family and friends baking desserts.
The collection’s emblematic prints, bright colors, unique textures and trims are inspired by an extravagant dessert menu of cupcakes, candy, cakes, and chocolates.
Centered around a core of swim and resort essentials, this collection includes one of a kind Swarvoski crystals in all of the brand’s best sellers. Angelys has taken her signature ruffles to another level with more acute details in the stitching and beads.
