The collection was designed with a nod to the Nafsika Skourti muse: an Arabian Millennial princess. Revealing elements such as voluminous sleeves, watercolor fabrics, sleek silhouettes, and corset tops, the collection embraces modern-day life with touches of opulence, harmonizing centuries-old Arab sensibilities with the nuances of the millennial princess life.
An exciting collaboration also came together for the Spring/Summer 2020 season, as New York based, Stylist and Creative Consultant, Rachael Wang partnered with Nafsika Skourti to bring the collection to life on a road trip through the Jordanian countryside.
