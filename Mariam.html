AW20/21 remains a continuation of our timeless story; an evolution of our classic silhouette. We continue to journey in the focus of refined tailoring with a contemporary twist.

The colours of the season: exuberant pinks, warm oranges, a fun turquoise contrasting a sophisticated black and white arise as a reflection of the interchanging seasonal moods, an atmospheric colour palette suited for the spirits of the season. Something Blue presents a curation of classic designs fit for the Mariam Al Sibai woman; daring and confident who values craft and quality.
