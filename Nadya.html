NADYA DZYAK

RTW

In the new spring-summer collection, designer Nadia Dzyak explores phenomenas of nature and trick with light. Light installations of the New York architectural studio Hou De Sousa and the aurora borealis are in the spotlight of designer’s mood board. Designer layers and mixes colors, creating the effect of simultaneous weightlessness and cohesiveness of images inspired by interweaving of shades in the sky.
Paying tribute to the 80s, Nadia continues to use famous shapes like high-waisted bottoms or voluminous sleeves, introducing brand’s iconic weightless organza dresses, decorated with asymmetric details. Traditionally, the brand pays special attention to manual work, using the unique technique of manually laying out pleated details and ruffles. The collection also comprises of pleated blouses paired with cargo pants, draped dresses with decorative rings and skirts with geometric cuts in shades of fuchsia, turquoise and fresh lime.
