NADYA DZYAK

RTW

The collection of new season Fall/Winter 2020/2021 reflects the inner world of the designer in full. Her heroine, inspired by the soap opera “Dynasty”, is an “iron woman”, making up her own games and playing by her own rules. Being bold and independent she is choosing bright colors both in clothes and makeup. Being a confident person she is not afraid of public attention. Animal prints, voluminous sleeves and pants, being kitsch attributes, are played with irony masterly by the designer Nadya Dzyak, transforming each look into an individual story of success.
The collection comprises of signature transparent dresses and blouses, made of weightless fabrics with the combination of geometric pleated elements and frills as well as geometric cuts and asymmetric silhouettes. Traditionally, the designer Nadya Dzyak uses intricate hand-stitched elements and bespoke sophisticated elements of décor while creating a new collection which define the DNA of the brand NADYA DZYAK.
