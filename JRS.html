Julien Riad Sahyoun

JEWELRY

Each collection tells a story, a sense of deep connection to the world, a way of sharing my values through my creations

Skin - Influenced by the designer’s mixed heritage, the Skin collection represents unity through tolerance and respect.

Star - The Star collection has been designed to illustrate human individuality. Everyone can make a difference by embracing their uniqueness.

Sun - The Sun collection pays tribute to Nature and the elements. This collection reveals a more ethereal side to the designer, whose Moroccan influences are seen in the delicate floral motifs of the pieces.

3ternity - 3ternity collection represents three essential values: Love, Friendship and Soul. These evocative words engraved onto the rim of each ring in French: Amour, Amitié, Âme.

Babylon - The design of this collection, has been influenced by the world’s earliest writing system known as cuneiform which evolved in Mesopotamia, later known as Babylon.
The jewellery describes the universe of passion in three words LOVE, PLAY and KILL through ancient symbolic writing.
Twiga -Will be on show for the first time at Maison Pyramide this month. Twiga is the Swahili word for giraffe - the jewellery features a giraffe skin pattern. In 2016 Julien took part in the Animal Ball which is part of the biggest animal charity in the Uk. Julien designed a giraffe mask which was displayed at the V&A in London. This inspired the Twiga collection and he will be planning to collaborate with the elephant family charity, part of the proceeds from sales of the collection will be donated to the charity.
